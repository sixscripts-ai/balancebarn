Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services, processes, and how we can help your business.

How quickly can you get started with my books?

Typically, we can have you up and running within 1-2 weeks. This includes the initial consultation, data migration, and QuickBooks setup. For urgent situations, we offer expedited onboarding within 3-5 business days.

What if I'm already using QuickBooks?

No problem! We'll review your existing setup, optimize your QuickBooks configuration, and ensure everything is organized properly. Our QuickBooks certified team can improve even well-maintained systems.

Do you work with businesses in my industry?

Yes! We work with businesses across all industries including retail, construction, professional services, e-commerce, healthcare, real estate, and more. Our team has experience with industry-specific accounting needs.

How do you ensure data security?

We use bank-level security with 256-bit SSL encryption, secure cloud-based solutions, and strict confidentiality protocols. All team members are background-checked and bound by confidentiality agreements.

What if I need to cancel my service?

You can cancel your service with written notice. We'll provide you with all your data in standard formats and ensure a smooth transition to maintain your business continuity.

Do you work with my existing accountant?

Absolutely! We often work alongside your existing accountant or tax preparer. We handle the day-to-day bookkeeping while they focus on tax planning and preparation. This collaboration often saves clients time and money.

How often will we communicate?

You'll receive monthly financial reports and have access to your books in real-time. We also conduct quarterly strategic reviews and are available for questions anytime via email or scheduled calls.

What makes you different from other bookkeeping services?

We're a family-owned business with Texas A&M educated team members, 100% QuickBooks certified, and we treat every client like family. Our combination of technical expertise and personal service is what sets us apart.
