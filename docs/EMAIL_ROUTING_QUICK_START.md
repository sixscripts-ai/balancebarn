# Quick Setup: Email Routing for support@thebalancebarn.com

## 🚀 Fast Track Setup (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Open Cloudflare Email Routing
👉 **Click here**: https://dash.cloudflare.com/9a5aca49515a11c70748d24c69b1b801/email/routing/overview

### Step 2: Enable Email Routing
1. Select domain: **thebalancebarn.com**
2. Click **Enable Email Routing**
3. Click **Add records and enable** (DNS records auto-added)

### Step 3: Add Your Email
1. Click **Destination addresses**
2. Click **Create address**
3. Enter: `aschtion2@gmail.com` (or your preferred email)
4. Click **Send**
5. ⚠️ **CHECK YOUR EMAIL** and click the verification link

### Step 4: Create support@ Address
1. Click **Routing rules** tab
2. Click **Create address**
3. Enter custom address: `support`
4. Select destination: your verified email
5. Click **Save**

### Step 5: Test It
1. Click **Send test email** in the dashboard
2. Send to: `support@thebalancebarn.com`
3. Check your inbox!

## ✅ That's It!

Your contact forms will now deliver emails to your inbox.
